Account Deletion

BearSlot Account Deletion Request

We understand you wish to delete your account and personal data. To process your request, please follow these steps:

1. Send an email to our support address:
tuncaranca@gmail.com

2. Use the following subject line in your email:
"Account Deletion Request"

3. Provide your details:
In the body of the email, please include the email address you used to register with BearSlot so we can identify your account.

Once we receive your request, we will proceed to permanently delete your account and all associated game data from our database within a reasonable timeframe. The data to be deleted includes your user ID, email address, roulette activations, total winnings, and any other game progress stored on our servers. We do not retain any personal user data after the deletion is processed.

Once we receive and process your request, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted. Please note that due to the manual nature of this process, you may not receive a separate email confirmation.
